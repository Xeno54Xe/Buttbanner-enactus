About Us

Ever wondered if your bad habits could save the planet? Introducing Butt Better, the revolutionary company turning discarded cigarette butts into eco-friendly treasures. Let's clear the streets, protect the environment, and make those pesky butts work for us.

Our Solution

Our innovative solution aims to tackle the pervasive problem of cigarette butt litter by implementing a comprehensive recycling initiative. This program focuses on collecting, processing, and repurposing cigarette butts into useful products, thereby reducing environmental pollution and promoting sustainability.

Our Impact

  • Reducing waste and pollution
  • Promoting sustainability
  • Transforming waste into valuable resources

Frequently Asked Questions

How do we collect cigarette butts?

We partner with local communities, organizations(bars, pubs etc), and volunteers to set up designated cigarette butt collection points in urban areas, parks, beaches, and other public spaces.

What products can we create from recycled cigarette butts?

We transform recycled materials into eco-friendly products, initially focused on using the fibrous material present in the butts to make the cigarette pack itself.

How can I get involved?

You can join our volunteer programs, participate in community clean-up events, or simply dispose of your cigarette butts responsibly at our collection points.
